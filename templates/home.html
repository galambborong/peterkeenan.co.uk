This site serves as a signpost for the various activities I'm involved with. You can read about my musical activities, my research or my music engraving projects. A few thoughts on this website specifically

I'm of the opinion that the world wide web is becoming increasingly flawed, dominated by the self-centred aims of tech giants and other controlling parties. For this reason, I am not on mainstream social media platforms (though I am on Mastodon), and hence I'm reverting to the notion held in the late 90s / early 00s in which one's website was one's primary outlet for sharing information and views.

I originally planned for this site to just be static html pages, but have taken the opportunity to learn a few new skills, so it is in fact written completely from the ground up in Python with the Flask interface. So, yes, it makes some very minimal use of databases, but there's no use of Wordpress or any other CMS. This aims to be a pretty minimal site, but I have created a parallel gopherhole, for those who just want the info with no bells and whistles!
