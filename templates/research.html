About my research

My research interests

My research interests cover a range of areas, primarily concerned with the interplay between a musical work, its materials, and its interpreter. This takes a few forms, including historically informed performance practice research and theories on the role of editing in contemporary music performance practice.

Lots of my research has centred around Mozart's music, in particular tempo theory. Naturally this goes in hand with my aims as a performer.
