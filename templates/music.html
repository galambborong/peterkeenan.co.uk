My musical activities

Opera dei Lumi

I founded Opera dei Lumi in 2014. We did two concert performances of Mozart operas (Così fan tutte and Le nozze di Figaro) and did a one-off performance of Mozart's Requiem in 2016. Realising that for this to really go somewhere (and not just be one-off things that were fun), I began building the organisation from the ground up in 2017. It is now a registered charity with a board of trustees, a clear business plan and – most importantly – an ever growing audience.

Northern Praeclassica

In January 2020 I took over as Music Director of Northern Praeclassica, a community a capella choir in Newcastle. I've worked with them for about a term, but due to covid-19 we've not yet performed together. (We would have done two concerts by now!) I'm looking forward to getting back to it.

My conducting

I've never really trained as a conductor but am grateful to get together with individuals I respect and discuss ideas.

Other

Once upon a time I played piano and viola, and studied composition too.
