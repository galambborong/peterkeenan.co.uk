Music engraving

Specialised music typography

Some of my work has been as a music engraver. I pretty much exclusively use SCORE, complemented by an array of third-party programs – most notably those by Tom Brodhead.

I have used Sibelius but cannot claim much expertise there (I do not have my own copy). Similarly, I bought Dorico eight or so months ago. I have played around with it a bit, but am yet to try a full project in it.

The music of Paul Keenan

My main activity these days is the editing and engraving of the music of Paul Keenan. The aim is to produce a set of performance materials with the same care and attention to detail as that of the Stockhausen-Verlag. This is a pretty hefty task, slowed down by the fact that it's not funded so I cannot commit consistent time to it. Nonetheless, I am making progress.
