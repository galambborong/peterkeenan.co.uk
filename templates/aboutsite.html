What is this site for?

Aims

This site is a signpost for the activities and I'm involved with and a place to share a few of my interests. You can read about my musical activities, my research or my music engraving projects. I've taken a recent interest in dev, so have also got my own git server, open for perusal (though there's not that much of interest, to be fair).

I'm thinking about blogging in some form from time to time, mainly airing my inconsistent philosophies regarding technology, privacy and similar issues.

Feel free to contact me directly if you want to know more about something and/or discuss anything you read here.

The web is becoming increasingly flawed. It is dominated by the self-centred aims of tech giants whose services we all use because of their alleged convenience. I am not on mainstream social mediia (though I am on Mastodon; I'm still making my mind up about whether or not I like it - though I support the concept). Thus, I'm using this site to revert to the late 90s / early 00s notion where one's website was one's primary outlet for sharing views, content and other information. Why should F*k, G*e and T*r profit from something I can just do myself?!

A little about this website build

This site is a simple bunch of static html pages (wonderful static html!), with a basic stylesheet.
That's about it..!
No CMS. No cookies. No analytics. No Javascript.

The original plan was to just write each page out, old school. Then I thought about going down the Hugo route. In the end, I decided to take the opportunity to learn a few new skills: This site has been created completely from the ground up using Python with the Flask interface. My local files make use of some json databases, but I then use Frozen-Flask to 'freeze' the site into static html pages, which I just upload to my server.
So simple! No need to even deploy Flask on my server!
It is just a bog standard minimal site, as that's all that is needed!
